Macro photography

My role

  • Research
  • Photography
  • Post production editing

Communication through photography is the course taken at Sheffield Hallam University. This project is about to show how images can send a message to the audience. The goal of this projects is to demonstrate to people that everyday life can be enjoyed with little treats such as sweets and candies. All the pictures are taken in a professional studio with all the photography equipment. External lights, macro lenses, post-production process. Editing is the biggest part of the projects. Photoshop and Lightroom are two software where the project was edited. Color correction and adjustments, art direction editing, etc are the biggest part of the final outcome. All of the images are stylized in the macro-genre to highlight the beauty of the objects and also make an attractive result. The final result is a photo book, which sends a message tells a story to people. Pictures itself shows the beauty on the one side and express themselves on the other side.

The Trumpet

My role

  • Research
  • Web design
  • Visual design

The Trumpet is a luxury e-commerce website selling fashion items. This project is the final assignment of the digital typography course. The main idea is to be focused on the typography but also to design a website layout while keeping the identity of it. The website has several pages every single page has its own content but all of them has a unique story which client see while going through pages. This is personal work created to the own idea and presented accordingly to the client ºs needs. The whole design is stylized in the soft color palette which makes the product rememberable for the users. The design is very simple on the one hand and on the other it is very informative and functional.
